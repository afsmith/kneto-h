Frequently asked questions

How long are your contracts?

For the single user plan: after your fifteen day trail your will be billed on a 30 day cycle and can cancel anytime. For the multi-user plans you will be billed in a 30 day cycle and you can cancel at anytime.

How do I sign up?

Click the ‘Order Now’ button. This will take you to a registration page, enter your information and we’ll send you a verification link in your email. Click that link, log in with your email address and your chosen password then select your plan and your done!

What is meant by unlimited?

You can send as many offers and proposals as you need. Keep in mind we are not a tool for bulk marketing communications. You should know who you are communicating with.

How does the trial work?

Sign up for the ‘Single User’ plan and try Kneto risk free for 15 days. If you don’t wish to continue, go to ‘Accounts’ and select ‘Cancel’. If you like it and want to have the rest of you team use it, upgrade to one of the ‘Early Access' plans.
